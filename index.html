ECHØ DATE is a five-member band from Germany rooted in indie rock. Their sound leans toward the soft and atmospheric rather than loud or heavy, blending warm guitars with honest, emotional lyrics. The band focuses on intimate, reflective melodies, leaving space for quiet moments as much as for catchy hooks. Their songs explore closeness, distance, and everything in between - personal stories shaped by melancholy, hope, and a subtle sense of forward motion.
